The Ask

Every vision, no matter how clear, needs fuel to turn into movement. SafeSpeed BlackBox was born from a single belief: that truth and safety should never be privileges reserved for a few, but rights accessible to everyone who steps into a car. Today, the idea has grown into something larger — a mission to rebuild trust between humans, machines, and institutions.

But to reach that mission, partnership is essential. I am seeking investors, mentors, and collaborators who see beyond numbers — who understand that this is not only a business, but a statement of progress and protection. The ask is not simply for funding; it is for alliance, guidance, and shared purpose.

Option A — Strategic License / IP Acquisition

A one-time $1,000,000 license or intellectual property acquisition, offering regional or global rights to deploy, manufacture, and scale SafeSpeed BlackBox technology under your brand. This option grants immediate entry into an expanding market of insurers, fleet operators, and automotive safety innovators. It is designed for companies that want to lead — not follow — in the evolution of intelligent road safety.

Option B — Pilot Partnership & Growth Investment

A €250,000 pilot fund to launch the first real-world deployment of 500–1,000 devices in collaboration with insurance partners and fleet networks. The outcome of this phase will not be a concept, but measurable data, case studies, and validation that define a new standard of evidence and fairness in road incidents.

Why Invest?

SafeSpeed BlackBox lives at the intersection of technology and morality — a bridge between what we can measure and what we must protect. It carries a simple truth: when accidents happen, data should speak louder than confusion. Supporting this project means joining the front line of ethical innovation — creating not only safer roads but a safer civilization.

The return is not just financial; it is generational. Your investment will help create systems that prevent loss, save lives, and empower millions of honest drivers who deserve fairness. The technology will open opportunities across insurance, mobility, fleet analytics, and smart-city infrastructure — industries ready for disruption and hungry for reliability.

Whether through partnership, acquisition, or collaboration, your support becomes part of a legacy — one that replaces doubt with truth, and transforms every vehicle from a moving object into a source of clarity. Together, we can make the road a place of confidence, not fear.

Two paths. One vision.
Choose to license the future — or build it with us.
