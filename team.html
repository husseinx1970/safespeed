Team

Behind every innovation there is a story — not of machines or money, but of a person who refused to look away from a problem that mattered. SafeSpeed BlackBox was born from that kind of determination.

Founder & Visionary

Hussein Hasan — Åmotfors, Sweden

I am currently the sole founder, designer, and developer behind SafeSpeed BlackBox. This project began as a personal mission — not to build a gadget, but to solve one of the most human problems of all: the fear and confusion that follow an accident, and the endless search for truth on the road.

Working alone has been both a challenge and a privilege. It allowed me to build the vision from the ground up, to shape every line of thought and design with one goal in mind — **to make technology serve fairness, not bureaucracy**. Every idea, prototype, and line of code carries the same fingerprint: a belief that truth is worth building for.

From my small workshop in Åmotfors, Sweden, I have designed, tested, and refined this system piece by piece, driven by curiosity, persistence, and a quiet conviction that one mind, if focused enough, can still move the world.

SafeSpeed BlackBox is not just a product — it is a reflection of a philosophy: that safety and honesty must be accessible to everyone, everywhere. And even though I am building this project alone today, I see a future where this idea gathers people from across the world — engineers, investors, dreamers, and believers — who share the same purpose.

“A single spark is enough to light a thousand roads.”

I am now seeking partners and collaborators who can help turn this vision into a global movement. Whether through investment, mentorship, or technical expertise — SafeSpeed BlackBox is ready to grow beyond one pair of hands.
